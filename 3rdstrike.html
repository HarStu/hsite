3rd strike

street fighter iii: 3rd strike is the final entry in capcom's street fighter iii series.

it's widely considered one of the best 2d fighters ever made. it's also my personal favorite.

i play 3rd strike, competitively, on-and-off. i'm hugely grateful for the efforts of the jazzy circuit, who've done an amazing job keeping the competitive fire going, 25+ years after the games' release.

i consider myself very intermediate. i play urien, and my best tournament placement is 17th at evo 2024

fighting games are fascinating and exciting. learning to become a competent 3rd strike player taught me a lot of hard-knock lessons about myself.
